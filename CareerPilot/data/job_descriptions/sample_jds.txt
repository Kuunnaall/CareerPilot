===== JOB DESCRIPTION 1: Senior Data Scientist =====

Company: TechCorp AI Solutions
Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)

We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our AI/ML team.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in machine learning and statistical modelling
- Strong proficiency in Python, TensorFlow or PyTorch, Scikit-learn
- Experience with NLP, text classification, and deep learning
- Hands-on with SQL, Spark, and big data pipelines
- Familiarity with Docker, AWS/GCP, and MLOps practices
- Experience with feature engineering, A/B testing, and model evaluation
- Strong communication and stakeholder management skills

Nice to have:
- Publications or Kaggle competition experience
- Knowledge of recommendation systems
- Experience with MLflow or Airflow

Responsibilities:
- Design and build production-grade ML models
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams
- Mentor junior data scientists
